Call for Papers

We invite high-quality, original, and unpublished papers addressing current issues, emerging trends, and innovative practices in science and technology education — with a focus on quality assurance, sustainability, and educational innovation.

Check Deadlines Registration Fees

Conference Topics

  • Sustainability in Science and Technology Education
  • Quality Assurance in Education
  • Curriculum Development
  • Distance Education
  • E-Learning and Digital Learning
  • Lifelong Learning
  • Teaching and Learning Innovation
  • Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET)

Submission (Overview)

  • Submissions must be original, unpublished, and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • Papers should be written in English and aligned with the conference themes and scope.
  • All submissions undergo peer-review by the Technical & Review Committee.
  • Accepted authors must register and present (physical or online).

Note: Paper template and online submission link will be announced on the official website.

Publication

All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings.

Selected high-quality papers will be invited for publication in Scopus-indexed journals (subject to review processes and publication requirements).
