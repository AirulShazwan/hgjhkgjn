General information

IC-BUSCLi 2026 serves as the umbrella gateway connecting two main conference streams under one shared platform and conference experience.

What is IC-BUSCLi?

A single entry point that brings together CSI and ICIBMAH. Visitors can immediately identify the right conference stream without navigating multiple disconnected pages.

Format & experience

Hybrid by design: attend physically at PWCC, Penang, or join online. The landing page is structured to feel premium, clean, and easy to explore.

Who should join?

Researchers, academics, practitioners, and postgraduate students across communication, language, business, engineering, technology, sustainability, and AI-related fields.

Tracks & clusters

5th CSI Tracks

Tracks 6–10 under CSI highlight communication, language, media, education, health, and AI-related inquiry.

1 • Education (Language & Pedagogy) 2 • Culture (Translation, Media & Society) 3 • Human-AI Interaction & User Experience 4 • Health Sciences (Wellbeing & Systems) 5 • Generative AI in Media Production

2nd ICIBMAH Tracks

Tracks 1–5 under ICIBMAH focus on business, engineering, humanities, and interdisciplinary perspectives.

6 • Business Management, Leadership & Entrepreneurship 7 • Accounting, Finance & Green Economics 8 • Engineering, Smart Tech & Applied Sciences 9 • Humanities, Society & Digital Ethics 10 • Interdisciplinary Education & Policy Studies
